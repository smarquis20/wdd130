Marquis Rafting

At Marquis Rafting, our purpose is to connect people with the raw power and beauty of nature through thrilling rafting experiences. We aim to create memories that last a lifetime while instilling a sense of responsibility towards the preservation of our environment. Our purpose encompasses education, conservation, and a commitment to sustainable practices, ensuring that future generations can also enjoy the wonders of our natural world.

History

Founded in the heart of the rugged wilderness in the early 2000s, Marquis Rafting emerged as a place for adventure seekers and nature enthusiasts alike. Starting with just a handful of seasoned guides and a couple of rafts, the company quickly gained recognition for its commitment to providing exhilarating rafting experiences on some of the most challenging and picturesque rapids in the region. Over the years, Marquis Rafting expanded its operations, investing in top-notch equipment, expanding its team of expert guides, and developing partnerships with local conservation organizations to promote environmental stewardship. Today, with a rich history of delivering unforgettable adventures while prioritizing safety and sustainability, Marquis Rafting continues to thrive as a premier destination for those seeking the ultimate adrenaline rush amidst the untamed beauty of the great outdoors.
