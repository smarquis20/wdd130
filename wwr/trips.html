Missouri River Trip

Description: The Missouri River offers a perfect introduction to river rafting for beginners. With Class I and II rapids, this trip provides gentle waves and easy maneuvering through scenic landscapes. The trip typically covers about 10 miles, starting from the Sunrise Bridge and ending at River Bend Park. Along the way, paddlers can enjoy views of the Missouri foothills.

$200

Osage River Trip

Description: The Osage River is renowned for its thrilling rapids and beautiful canyon scenery, making it an ideal choice for intermediate rafters seeking a bit more excitement. This trip features Class III and IV rapids, including exciting drops and challenging obstacles. The journey spans approximately 8 miles. Rafters can expect continuous action as they navigate through narrow gorges.

$400

Mississippi River Trip

Description: For experienced rafters seeking the ultimate adventure, the Mississippi River offers Class IV and V rapids amidst stunning Missouri wilderness. Known for its turquoise waters and powerful currents, this trip provides an adrenaline rush like no other. The expedition typically covers 30 miles over multiple days, with campsites nestled along the riverbanks.

$800
